Other Services

Bag Inspection and Makeup Lesson

Together we’ll go through you makeup bag and decide what’s working for you and what’s working against you. Next, we’ll create a look that you can easily achieve on you own with a combination of your products and some help from my extensive makeup kit. I’ll apply makeup to half of your face and-with my guidance- you’ll recreate the look on the other side. We’ll work together until we get it right. By the end of our session you’ll feel confident in your own application abilities. I’ll also give you a list of products you can purchase to fill out your makeup bag (I’m totally unbiased when it comes to brand, I just fill you in on what I know works).

  • $150 (allow at least two hours)

The Complete Package

Ready for a day of beauty and fun? First we’ll go through your makeup bag and toss whatever isn’t working for you. I’ll fill you in on what colors and products work best for your skin tone and any skin conditions you have. From there we’ll head out on a makeup shopping adventure (I can easily work within your budget). When we’re finished your makeup bag will perfectly tailored to you. Next we’ll head back and try it all out. You can choose to have a full application done by me with detailed instructions or I can apply makeup to half of your face and you can recreate the same look on the other side.

  • $350. – Full Day (about 6 hours but there is no time limit)
  • $400 – Split days (You can choose to split up the service over two days. $200 is due on each day of service. Days so not need to be consecutive.)
